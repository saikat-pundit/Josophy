**AVOID:** **Long-duration nominal fixed-rate bonds.** The 30-year yield's consistent upward trend (+17 bps annualized) and the strong inflationary backdrop imply significant capital depreciation risk. The risk-reward for locking in long-term fixed rates is unfavorable.
